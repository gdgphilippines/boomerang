About the Event

TensorFlow Dev Summit was held on March 30, 2018 in Mountain View, California. TensorFlow Dev Summit brings together a diverse mix of machine learning users from around the world for a full day of highly technical talks, demos, and conversation with the TensorFlow team and community.

GDG Philippines is organizing a TensorFlow Dev Summit 2018 Extended Manila.
