About the Event

Actions on Google and Google Assistant are one of the major Google technical areas and we want to invite the whole developer community to learn about creating Actions for the Google Assistant.

“Build Actions for Your Community” is an open and modular event series designed by Google and offered to community organizers worldwide. You can offer opportunities to developers attending your events to learn how to build Actions for the Google Assistant that are useful for their community - whether it's a professional network, interest group, or your hometown. This way, they can build something in an area that they care about or are interested in.
