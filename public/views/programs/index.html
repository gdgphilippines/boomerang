About

The Campus Roadshow program is our initiative to help universities and schools be equip with new skills and knowledge. The program is good for a day with talks and hands-on workshops on various technologies may it be on Android, Web, Cloud, Virtual Reality, Artificial Intelligence and others.

Speakers

Interact and learn directly from the local experts.

What to prepare?

  • A conducive venue for learning

    For the morning talks, we suggest a venue that is able to accommodate your participants. For the afternoon breakout workshops, we suggest computer laboratories, if none is available, a classroom slash bring-your-own-device set-up will work too.

  • Fast and stable internet connection

    Though we can adjust the talks and workshop to support offline, it would be best if you have a good connection to allow speakers to demonstrate interactive technologies.

  • Transportation and accommodation

    Since we are a volunteer group, our programs are funded by our partners or school host.
